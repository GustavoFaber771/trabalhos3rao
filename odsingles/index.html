Para que o ODS-11 serve?

  • SDG 11 aims to ensure that cities and communities are developed sustainably, considering aspects such as infrastructure, urban planning, access to adequate housing, transport, basic services, public spaces, security, resilience to natural disasters, among others.





Explanatory video about SDG-11




Some of the key themes addressed by SDG 11 include:

Sustainable urban planning: promote adequate planning of cities, considering population growth, equitable distribution of resources and preservation of the environment.

Access to adequate housing: ensuring that all people have access to safe, affordable and sustainable housing, especially for vulnerable groups such as the poor and homeless.

Sustainable transport: improving public transport infrastructure, promoting sustainable modes of transport and reducing dependence on private vehicles, contributing to the reduction of air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions.

Preservation of cultural and natural heritage: protecting and preserving the cultural and natural heritage of cities, promoting sustainable tourism and equitable access to public spaces.
